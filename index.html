Why Choose EcoWipe?

Door Step Service

Our service provides the convinience of daily car wash and the quality of professional car cleaning right at your door step.

Eco-Friendly

Our service is designed ground up to save water and be healthy for the environment by using less than a glass of water.

Professional

Our car washers are trained to give the cleaning your car deserves and the care never recieved.

Shinier

There are polymers in our product that provide a protective layer, giving a wax finish. The end result being that your car will have that show room shine and you only have consumed the equivalent of one cup of water.

Rust Free

In traditional washing of vehicles with water we are introducing water into places we cannot reach to dry where it could accelerate a pre-existing rust problem or formation of rust due to stagnant residue water. The proactive way to prevent future rust when it comes to cleaning is to use a waterless wash. Besides helping to prevent the formation of rust in areas we cannot see or reach.

Scratch Free

By using different high quality microfibre cloths for tyres, body and glasses we ensure your car surface is prevented from scratches and other swirl marks that otherwise damage the surface of the vehicle.

Paint Protection

The normal water wash which mostly is hard water of STPs or borewells used for cleaning damages the car surface by reacting with the paint causing it to fall dull. By going waterless you not only prevent this but our unique solutions having active polymers with caranuba wax giving your car surface a protective film and helping keeping your car shiny.

Cleaner Society

Your traditional bucket wash left huge amounts of water on the ground which not only made the parking lot and your society look dirty but also huge amount of stagnant water acts as breeding ground for mosquitoes and other insects raising health and hygiene concerns. With our waterless car wash service there is absolutely zero waer spillage ensuring a cleaner and healthier society.

Glass Care

We often complain about the difficulty we have driving our car at night because the glasses are not cleaned properly or watermarks caused due to drying of water is visible barring our vision. With our service we pay special attention to glasses and by using a specific microfibre cloth we ensure that you have a clean and shiny glass.
